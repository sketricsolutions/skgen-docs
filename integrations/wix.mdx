  <Tab title="Floating Chat Bubble">

  ## **Step 1: Create your SketricGen account**

    To get started with the integration, **[sign in to your SketricGen account](https://app.sketricgen.ai)**. If you don’t have an account yet, you can create one for **[free](https://app.sketricgen.ai)**. Once you're logged in, begin setting up your chatbot by uploading your data sources. These can include files, text snippets, websites, or Q&A pairs, which will serve as the knowledge base for your chatbot.

    If you need assistance with setting up a working SketricGen chatbot, refer to the **[step-by-step guide](https://docs.sketricgen.ai/user-guide/creating-your-first-agent)** for creating and deploying your chatbot.
    
    ## **Step 2: Copy the SketricGen Chatbot Embed Code**

  Go to your chatbot dashboard, click **Publish**, and copy the embed code.  
  **Note**: Ensure your chatbot is set to **Public**.

  ![](/images/wix/screenshot1.png)

  ## **Step 3: Embed the Chatbot in Wix**

  ### 1. Log in to your Wix dashboard.  
  ### 2. Select the site where you want the chatbot.

     ![](/images/wix/screenshot2.png)

  ### 3. Go to **Add Elements > Embed & Social > Embed Code**.

     ![](/images/wix/screenshot3.png)

  ### 4. Paste the copied code and adjust position/size as needed.

     ![](/images/wix/screenshot4.png)

  ### 5. Add an **Anchor** and **Pin** the chatbot widget to make it a floating bubble.

     ![](/images/wix/screenshot5.png)

  ## **Step 4: Test the Chatbot**

  After publishing, test the chatbot on your live site.

  ![](/images/wix/screenshot6.png)

  ✅ **Success!** Your chatbot is live as a floating bubble on Wix.
